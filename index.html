Harassment


Harassment, often of a sexual nature, is a civil offense, meaning perpetrators cannot be criminally charged and punished. Cyberharassment, or telecommunications harassment in many states, is a criminal offense. Cyberharassment usually pertains to threatening or harassing email messages, instant messages, or to blog entries or websites dedicated soely to formaenting an individual.
